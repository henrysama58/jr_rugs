Info

There will be limited availability for commissions each week.

A Google Form will be posted every other Sunday at 12PM with a limit of five slots.

Rugs will take approximately 3-4 business days depending on the size and detail.

Please make commissions managable. I am NOT Picasso, so the images will be simplified and the colors may not be exact.

Also please be patient as I am only one person, and can only do so much.

Thank you!
